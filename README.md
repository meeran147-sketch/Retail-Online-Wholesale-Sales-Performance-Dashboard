# Retail-Online-Wholesale-Sales-Performance-Dashboard

## Project objective
The primary objective of this **Retail, Online & Wholesale Sales Performance Dashboard** is to analyze and monitor sales performance across multiple sales channels using interactive data visualizations. The dashboard is designed to track key business metrics such as total sales, orders, quantity sold, and unit prices while providing insights into regional and product category performance. It helps identify sales trends, compare performance across different regions and channels, and evaluate monthly revenue patterns. By transforming raw sales data into meaningful charts, KPIs, and reports, the dashboard enables users to make informed business decisions, uncover growth opportunities, and improve overall sales strategy through data-driven analysis.

### Process 
- Gathered sales data containing information on orders, sales channels, regions, product categories, salespersons, quantities sold, and revenue.
- Removed duplicates, handled missing values, corrected data formats, and ensured data consistency for accurate analysis.
- Structured the cleaned data into Excel tables and prepared it for analysis using Pivot Tables.
- Calculated key performance indicators (KPIs) such as Total Sales, Total Orders, Quantity Sold, and Average Unit Price.
- Analyzed sales performance by region, sales channel, product category, salesperson, and monthly trends.
- Developed Pivot Charts, KPI cards, and visualizations to represent sales insights effectively.
- Added slicers and filters to allow users to dynamically explore data by region, channel, category, and salesperson.
- Arranged all visuals into a professional dashboard layout and validated results to ensure accuracy and usability.

### Project Insights
- **Retail sales channel generated the highest revenue**, indicating stronger customer engagement and contribution to overall business growth.
- **The West region recorded the highest sales performance**, while some regions showed opportunities for improvement and market expansion.

- **Technology products emerged as the top-performing category**, contributing a significant share of total sales revenue.
- **Sales performance showed noticeable monthly fluctuations**, highlighting seasonal demand patterns and peak sales periods.
- **A small group of top salespersons contributed a large portion of total sales**, emphasizing the importance of high-performing sales teams.
- **Online and Wholesale channels demonstrated consistent growth**, suggesting potential for increased investment in digital and bulk sales strategies.
- **Interactive filtering revealed distinct customer and product trends**, enabling more targeted business decisions and performance monitoring.
- **The dashboard transformed complex sales data into actionable insights**, helping stakeholders identify opportunities, optimize strategies, and improve overall business performance.

## Final Conclusion

The Retail, Online & Wholesale Sales Performance Dashboard successfully transformed raw sales data into meaningful and actionable business insights. Through the use of KPIs, interactive charts, and dynamic filters, the dashboard provided a clear view of sales performance across different channels, regions, products, and salespersons. The analysis identified top-performing areas, highlighted sales trends, and revealed opportunities for business growth and optimization. Overall, this dashboard demonstrates how data visualization and analytical techniques in MS Excel can support effective decision-making, improve performance monitoring, and help organizations develop data-driven sales strategies.
